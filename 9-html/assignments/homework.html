About Rieslings
Riesling is a German wine. An aromatic grape variety displaying flowery, almost perfumed, aromas as well as high acidity.
It is used to make dry, semi-sweet, sweet, and sparkling white wines.
Riesling wines are usually varietally pure and are seldom oaked.

Riesling Varieties different types of Riesling include:
  • super sweet (Trockenbeerenauslese) - sweetest of Riesling varieties
  • late harvest Riessling - made from grapes that remain on the vine beyond harvest, where they continue to sweeten
  • Riesling ice wines - a desert wine
  • Botrys Riesling wines - fruity and spicy
